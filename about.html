About Me.

My name is Nolan Hartwig and I’m a producer and graphic designer from Michigan. I grew up with a passion for creating, whether it was art, music, or projects in the woodshop, I always had my hands and mind focused on making something new. As I grew older I developed a love for music and graphic design and now work to produce music for small artists and aid them with all of their graphic design needs.

My graphic design experience goes past the courses, books, and education that went into reaching where I’m at today. I have done client work and received gold in districts and regionals, as well as 6th place in states for the annual SkillsUSA Graphic T-Shirt design competition. My work has been displayed as the logos of multiple internet personalities amassing over a total of 250,000 followers. I am also proficient in Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and InDesign, along with Premiere Pro for video work.

I have been producing electron music for about 5 years. I am self-taught with studio experience at Above Pete’s Garage Audio and Visual Productions which is based in the Gary Gray Institute. I have worked with several small artists and many casual clients interested in learning about and creating music. I aim to use my skills in music to create a uplifting message throughout the rap game rather than the many messages of violence and drug abuse which has so greatly corrupted our the youth.

My passions and hobbies include boxing, calisthenics, chess, and business. I plan on attending Northern Michigan University to study business and marketing starting in fall of
